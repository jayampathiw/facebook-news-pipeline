# ItaliaOggi — 1000 Views/Day Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-21
**Goal:** 1000 organic views per day on ItaliaOggi Facebook page

---

## Audience Profile

### Who we have

Facebook Insights shows the audience is heavily concentrated in Rome with strong presence across all major Italian cities:

| City / Region | Share | Character |
|---|---|---|
| Rome | 31% | Capital city, older demographic, Catholic, community-oriented |
| Genova | ~8% | Port city, working class and retirees, strong local identity |
| Milan | ~8% | Northern Italy, pragmatic, cost-of-living conscious |
| Turin | ~7% | Northern industrial city, proud heritage |
| Naples | ~6% | Southern identity, family-centric, strong Catholic tradition |
| Palermo | ~5% | Sicilian identity, justice-conscious, Mediterranean values |

**Dominant profile: women aged 65+, concentrated in Rome, present across all major Italian cities.**

### Who they are

Retired women who lived through the anni di piombo, the economic miracles, and Italy's transformation. They are:
- **Deeply Catholic** — Vatican News is a trusted source
- **Family-first** — their reference frame is grandmother, mother, community
- **Justice-sensitive** — they remember when justice was denied and celebrate when it's served
- **Health-anxious** — medication costs, hospital access, chronic conditions affect daily life
- **Cost-of-living watchers** — pension income, grocery prices, utility bills dominate concerns
- **Emotionally driven sharers** — they share what moves them, not what informs them

### What they share

- Stories with named victims and specific ages ("Marta, 72 anni, di Roma")
- Health warnings that affect them or their family directly
- Justice served — court verdicts, sentences, vindication stories
- Vatican and faith news
- Prices going up (food, medicine, utilities)
- Crimes against elderly people or children
- Stories of human resilience and solidarity

### What they do NOT share

- Partisan political debates (coalition politics, parliament maneuvers)
- NATO/geopolitics with no family angle
- Complex fiscal policy without pension/grocery impact
- Tech culture, social media trends, youth lifestyle
- Abstract economic statistics without human cost

## The 4-Layer Content Model

### Layer 1 — Serve Rome + Major Cities Hard (Retention)

**Pillar:** `la-mia-citta`
**Frequency:** 2 posts/week (increased from 1)
**Posting slot:** 19:30 CEST

With Rome at 31% of the audience, local Roman news is the highest-retention content available. A story about a court verdict in Rome, a hospital closure in the city, or a local elder fraud case will be shared by people who feel personally connected to that place.

**What to post:**
- Rome court verdicts and local justice stories
- Hospital and healthcare access in Rome/major cities
- Local cost-of-living news (Rome grocery prices, utility changes)
- Crime affecting elderly or families in major cities
- Local religious events, parish news, community solidarity

**Sources:** Roma Today RSS (added to pipeline), Il Messaggero (already present, Rome-focused)

**Signal for approval:** Would a 70-year-old woman in Trastevere forward this to her daughter?

---

### Layer 2 — National with 65+ Female Resonance (Acquisition)

**Pillars:** `fatto-del-giorno`, `italia-nel-mondo`, `italia-che-funziona`
**Frequency:** 9 posts/week
**Posting slots:** 07:30, 11:30, 19:30 CEST

National news framed through the lens of what it means for a retired woman's daily life. The same pension reform story lands completely differently when the hook is "Le pensioni aumentano di 47€ al mese — quanto cambia per voi?" versus "Il governo approva la legge finanziaria".

**What to post:**
- Pension and social security changes (direct financial impact)
- Healthcare system news: reimbursements, waiting lists, drug prices
- Food price changes: seasonal agricultural reports, supermarket price surveys
- Italy seen from abroad: stories that make them proud of their country
- Positive stories of Italian excellence and solidarity

**The framing rule:** First sentence must answer "cosa cambia per me?" not "cosa ha deciso il governo?"

---

### Layer 3 — Emotional Viral Content (Growth)

**Pillar:** `storie-italiane`
**Frequency:** 2 posts/week (unblocked and increased from 1)
**Best slot:** 19:30 CEST

These are the posts that travel far beyond existing followers. A story about an elderly woman defrauded in Naples gets shared by women in Rome, Milan, and Palermo — all of whom see themselves in that story.

**What to post:**
- Named-victim stories: elder fraud, medical negligence, family tragedy (always name+age)
- Justice delivered: court sentences for crimes against vulnerable people
- Human solidarity: neighbours helping neighbours, community support stories
- Family safety warnings: new scam methods targeting elderly, unsafe products
- Health alerts: medication recalls, disease outbreaks, hospital safety issues

**The named-victim rule:** If the article names the victim with age — "Carmela, 74 anni, di Napoli" — it will outperform any equivalent institutional article by 3–5×. The hook must lead with the person, not the institution.

**Emoji guide for Layer 3:**
- 🙏 solidarity and faith
- ❤️ family and emotion  
- ⚠️ health and safety alerts
- 🕊️ deaths and tributes
- Avoid: 🗳️ 🏛️ (political signals repel this audience)

---

### Layer 4 — Sports (Match-Day Engine)

**Pillar:** `azzurri`
**Frequency:** 3 posts/week
**Posting slot:** 15:30 CEST

Already fully implemented. Covers Nazionale Italiana (football and rugby), Serie A, Coppa del Mondo, major Italian clubs. The 65+ audience engages with national pride sports (Azzurri) more than club football — frame results as national moments, not club victories.

**Primary coverage:**
- Nazionale Italiana (football) — universal Italian pride
- Nazionale di rugby — growing female audience engagement
- Serie A finals and decisive matches — Juventus, Inter, Milan, Napoli
- Major Italian athletes (Sinner in tennis, Italian cyclists)
- Roma/Lazio for the 31% Rome audience

## Path to 1000 Views/Day

| Layer | Posts/week | Expected reach per post | Weekly views |
|---|---|---|---|
| Layer 1 — Rome/cities local | 2 | 250–500 (loyal local audience shares) | 500–1000 |
| Layer 2 — National/65+ framed | 9 | 50–200 (organic discovery) | 450–1800 |
| Layer 3 — Emotional viral | 2 | 400–1000 (shares travel far) | 800–2000 |
| Layer 4 — Sports (azzurri) | 3 | 200–500 (national pride shares) | 600–1500 |
| **Total/week** | **16** | | **2350–6300** |
| **Daily average** | | | **335–900 views/day** |

The upper range clears 1000. The gap at the lower end closes with:

1. **Named-victim discipline** — every Layer 3 post must have a named protagonist with age. This single change is worth 3–5× engagement multiplier.
2. **Vatican/faith content timing** — Vatican News posts perform best on Sunday morning. The 07:30 Sunday slot should always prioritize `vaticano` pillar.
3. **Image generation for Layer 1 and 3** — local city stories and emotional stories with a face or place image get 3× the shares of text-only posts.

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Post for ItaliaOggi)

| Content type | Why it fails |
|---|---|
| Coalition politics (centrodestra vs centrosinistra) | Audience is apolitical — they vote by family tradition, not ideology |
| Abstract EU/NATO/fiscal policy | No daily-life angle |
| Celebrity gossip (entertainment scene) | Wrong demographic entirely |
| Youth culture, TikTok, influencers | Wrong age group |
| Crime with no named victim | Institutional crime reporting gets scrolled past |
| Sports results with no national pride angle | Club tribalism matters less than Azzurri pride for 65+ women |
| Long legal/constitutional analysis | `capire-la-legge` works only in carousel format (not yet built) |

## Operational Checklist (Before Approving an Article)

- [ ] Is there a named person (victim, protagonist, beneficiary) in the hook?
- [ ] Is the first sentence answering "cosa cambia per me?" not "cosa ha deciso il parlamento?"
- [ ] For health articles: is the impact on daily life (drugs, hospital, costs) explicit?
- [ ] Has an image been generated?
- [ ] For sports: is this about the Nazionale or a major Azzurri moment (not just a club result)?
- [ ] Would a 70-year-old grandmother in Rome share this with her daughter on WhatsApp?

If the answer to the last question is no, reject regardless of other signals.

## Sunday Protocol

Sunday is the highest-engagement day for this audience (retired, home, more Facebook time).

- 07:30 slot: always prioritize `vaticano` pillar — Vatican Sunday mass, Pope message
- 12:00 slot: `italia-che-funziona` — positive week-in-review or uplifting story
- 19:30 slot: `storie-italiane` — human interest story with emotional resonance
