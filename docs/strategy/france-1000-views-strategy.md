# FranceAujourdhui — 1000 Views/Day Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-21
**Goal:** 1000 organic views per day on FranceAujourdhui Facebook page

---

## Audience Profile

### Who we have

Facebook Insights shows 55% of followers are concentrated in five cities:

| City | Share | Character |
|---|---|---|
| Marseille | 23% | OM-passionate, port city working class and retirees, strong local identity |
| Avignon | 8% | Festival city, mixed demographics, southern values |
| Hyères | 8% | Retirement destination, older demographic, Mediterranean lifestyle |
| Nice | 8% | RN-led city, security-conscious, Côte d'Azur identity, OGC Nice fans |
| Perpignan | 8% | RN-led city, Catalan border identity, RC Toulon/Stade Toulousain rugby territory |

**Combined PACA/Occitanie: ~55% of total audience.**

The remaining 45% is national French audience with no confirmed concentration.

### Who they are

Retired and semi-retired residents of Mediterranean France. Predominantly 55+. Share content about:
- Local security and justice (faits divers, court verdicts)
- Health risks and medical costs
- Mediterranean cost of living (food prices, tourism economy)
- Regional identity and pride
- Sport as community identity (OM, OGC Nice, RC Toulon, rugby)
- National news that directly impacts their daily life

### What they do NOT share

- Paris insider political debates between parties
- NATO/defense policy abstractions
- Complex fiscal policy without direct local impact
- Content aimed at under-40 urban demographics
- Partisan memes

## The 4-Layer Content Model

### Layer 1 — Serve PACA Hard (Retention)

**Pillar:** `ma-ville-aujourdhui`
**Frequency:** 2 posts/week
**Posting slot:** 19:00 CEST

Content that the existing 55% share with neighbours and family. These people already follow you — the goal is to make them ambassadors.

**What to post:**
- Local Provence/Côte d'Azur/Occitanie news
- Regional infrastructure (roads, hospitals, housing)
- Mediterranean weather and environment events
- Local economy (tourism numbers, property prices, fishing, agriculture)
- Regional culture and heritage

**Sources:** La Provence, Nice-Matin, Midi Libre (all added to pipeline)

**Signal for approval:** Does a retired Marseillais forward this to their neighbour? If yes, approve.

---

### Layer 2 — National with Regional Resonance (Acquisition)

**Pillars:** `france-en-debat`, `le-monde-vu-paris`, `fierte-francaise`
**Frequency:** 17 posts/week
**Posting slots:** 07:30, 12:00, 19:00 CEST

Topics that matter everywhere but framed from a southern perspective. Parisians scroll past these — southerners share them because they feel seen.

**What to post:**
- Pension reform (direct financial impact)
- Health system coverage and hospital closures
- Food price increases and grocery costs
- Summer tourism economy (impacts their region's income)
- National security stories with regional angle
- Excellence françaises (achievements that make them proud)

**The framing rule:** Always answer "what changes for people in Marseille/Nice/Avignon?" in the first sentence. Do not open with a Paris institution.

---

### Layer 3 — Emotion-Driven Viral Content (Growth)

**Pillar:** `france-en-debat` (faits divers and justice subset)
**Frequency:** 2–3 posts/week (included within the Layer 2 quota)
**Best slots:** 07:30 and 19:00 CEST

These cross all regional lines. A story about an elderly woman defrauded in Lyon gets shared by women in Marseille, Avignon, and Nice.

**What to post:**
- Named-victim tragedies (elder fraud, medical negligence, violent crime)
- Justice stories (verdicts delivered, sentences handed down)
- Family safety (child safety, scams targeting retirees)
- Human interest with a protagonist the audience can identify with

**The named-victim rule:** If the article names the victim with an age (e.g., "Mireille, 72 ans"), it will outperform an equivalent institutional article by 3–5×. Always include the name and age in the hook.

---

### Layer 4 — Sports (Match-Day Engine)

**Pillar:** `sport-francais`
**Frequency:** 3 posts/week
**Posting slots:** 12:00 (pre-match preview) and 19:00 (post-match reaction)

Sports is not entertainment for the PACA audience — it is community identity. An OM victory post is shared by people who do not even follow the news page, purely because it represents their city.

**Primary clubs and coverage:**

| Club | Sport | Audience connection | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| Olympique de Marseille (OM) | Football | Marseille = 23% of audience | ★★★★★ |
| OGC Nice | Football | Nice = 8% of audience | ★★★★☆ |
| RC Toulon | Rugby | PACA flagship rugby club, European champions | ★★★★☆ |
| Stade Toulousain | Rugby | Occitanie region, most dominant French rugby club | ★★★☆☆ |
| Équipe de France | Football/Rugby | National pride, universal engagement | ★★★★☆ |
| Tour de France | Cycling | PACA/Pyrenees stages, annual peak moment | ★★★★☆ |
| Roland-Garros | Tennis | National prestige, June annual peak | ★★★☆☆ |
| Montpellier HSC | Football | Midi Libre territory | ★★☆☆☆ |

**Hook format for sports:**
- Victory: `"L'OM s'impose 2-1 — Marseille peut souffler 🔵⚪"`
- Defeat: `"Défaite de l'OM — Marseille retient son souffle pour la suite ⚽"`
- Derby: `"OM-OGC Nice ce soir — le derby méditerranéen qui passionne le Sud 🔥"`

**Timing rule:** Post-match reactions must be published within 2 hours of final whistle to benefit from Facebook's algorithm boost on current sports content. The 19:00 slot covers most Ligue 1 matches (17:00–21:00 CEST kick-offs).

**Image rule:** All sports posts must have an image generated before approval. An OM match post with a crowd/stadium composite gets 3–5× the shares of text-only.

**Source:** L'Équipe RSS feed (added to pipeline) — covers all of the above automatically.

## Path to 1000 Views/Day

| Layer | Posts/week | Expected reach per post | Weekly views |
|---|---|---|---|
| Layer 1 — PACA local | 2 | 200–400 (loyal audience shares) | 400–800 |
| Layer 2 — National/regional | 14–15 | 50–150 (organic discovery) | 700–2250 |
| Layer 3 — Viral emotion | 2–3 | 300–800 (shares travel far) | 600–2400 |
| Layer 4 — Sports | 3 | 300–600 (match-day shares) | 900–1800 |
| **Total/week** | **21–23** | | **2600–7250** |
| **Daily average** | | | **370–1035 views/day** |

The upper range already clears 1000. The lower range requires improving post quality on two dimensions:

1. **Image generation for every approved article** — especially sports and Layer 1 local stories. Text-only posts consistently underperform image posts by 3–5×.
2. **Match-day timing discipline** — sports posts approved and scheduled within 2h of a match result hit the algorithm peak. Posts approved the next morning get 40–60% fewer views.

## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Post)

These consistently underperform with the PACA audience and should be rejected or deprioritized:

| Content type | Why it fails |
|---|---|
| Paris political insider debate (LFI vs RN vs LREM) | Audience is non-partisan regionalists |
| NATO/defense spending abstractions | No local relevance |
| Complex EU fiscal policy | Too abstract, no Mediterranean angle |
| Celebrity gossip (Paris entertainment scene) | Wrong demographic |
| Youth culture / TikTok trends | Wrong age group |
| Sports from outside France with no national team angle | Low relevance to this audience |

---

## Operational Checklist (Before Approving an Article)

- [ ] Does the hook name a person, place, or result — not an institution?
- [ ] Is there a PACA city or club name in the first sentence?
- [ ] Has an image been generated?
- [ ] Is the sport post within 2h of match conclusion (if match-day)?
- [ ] Would a retired woman in Marseille forward this to a friend?

If the answer to the last question is no, reject regardless of other signals.
